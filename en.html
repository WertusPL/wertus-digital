FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before starting the cooperation.

Cooperation starts with a short conversation or message where we learn about your business, offer, target customers and website goals. Based on that, we prepare a proposed scope, estimated cost and action plan. This way, you know from the beginning what will be done and how the process will look.

A standard website project usually takes between 7 and 21 business days. The exact timeline depends on the project scope, number of subpages, availability of materials and how quickly each stage is approved. For larger websites, the schedule is agreed individually.

Prices start from 1500 PLN for a landing page and from 3000 PLN for a business website. Every project is quoted individually because the final price depends on the number of sections, subpages, features, level of design detail and possible post-launch support. Before starting, you receive a clear quote with no hidden costs.

Yes. You do not need to have everything ready from the start. We help organize the website structure, plan the most important sections and define what materials are needed. We can also advise how to present your offer in a clearer and more sales-focused way.

Yes. As part of the project, we take care of basic technical SEO: proper heading structure, meta title, meta description, loading speed, responsiveness and correct section linking. This provides a solid foundation for future positioning and visibility in Google.

Yes. After publication, we can provide ongoing technical care, small improvements, updates, backups and further website development. This means you are not left alone with technical matters, and the website can grow together with your business.

Yes. The website can be expanded with additional subpages, case studies, offer sections, a blog, language versions, forms, integrations or extra features. In most cases, we start with a strong foundation and then develop the website according to the company's needs.
